Launching the "Jordan Teachers" platform

The Ministry of Education Launches a Training Program for Male and Female Teachers "via distance learning" via the "Jordan Teachers" platform www.teachers.gov.jo The Ministry has published a demonstration video of the registration mechanism and entry to the platform

31 March

Postponement of premium housing for a decent living for workers in the Ministry of Education

The Minister of Education, Dr. Tayseer Al-Nuaimi, decided to postpone the installments of a decent residence for a decent living for workers in the Ministry of Education, for the current month of April.This decision comes from the Ministry to reduce the financial burden on the children of the educational family in light of the conditions related to the emerging corona virus.
